Oh photography; how modern technology has bastardized you as an artform. While I may not hold divine photo talent or equipment, I muster up what I have to create images to compliment the projects I'm working on.

 

Studio Studies

Canon 5D MK1 / Studio Lighting

This set contains images taken in a studio. The objective was to learn how to manipulate artificial lights in a controlled enviroment to create the shots you want every time. Softboxes, spotlights, and smith-vectors were used. No strobes. Props were self-provided.

View This Image Set

Barbarella

Nikon D40

To create graphic assets that I could use for a brochure and website that I was designing for Barbarella Beauty: Boutique & Salon, I went to the salon to take photos. I focused on capturing the atmosphere and mood of the place by paying attention to the purple hue of the walls.

View This Image Set

Blissmobox

Pentax K110D

Blissmo dot com sells monthly, subscription-based boxes of organic/sustainable goods, whose contents are unknown to the recipient. To document each months' boxes, and to use for future selling opportunities, I tried to capture each box in as many different environments as possible (mostly outdoors).

View This Image Set

Color at Night

Pentax K110D

In this set of images, the goal was to study and explore the colors seen in the night time. The area seen in the photos takes place at the main park in downtown San Jose. Armed with only a camera, tripod, and a few props, I ventured into the park alone. These images tell a narrative story of a detective arriving at a crime scene.

View This Image Set
